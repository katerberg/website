## 🍿 Media Consumption

## 🎲 Games

- Video games have largely not been monopolizing my life, but [Nuclear Throne](https://store.steampowered.com/app/242680/Nuclear_Throne/) got quite a bit of play, despite it being an old game and very very hard for me. ## 🧠 Thinking About

- Slightly cheating to just be replaying what I'm doing in therapy, but I am learning to incorporate distance between reaction and response to situations. When something goes sideways, I have tended to just snap whatever I was feeling and am trying to instead recognize that impulse and think about how I will have wanted to respond. This is a continuous learning process, but when I succeed in taking a breath, it has felt like a super power.
